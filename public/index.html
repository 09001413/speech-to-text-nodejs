Speech to Text

TThe IBM Watson Speech to Text service uses speech recognition capabilities to convert English, Japanese, and Spanish speech into text. The transcription of incoming audio is continuously sent back to the client with minimal delay, and it is corrected as more speech is heard. The service can be accessed via a REST interface.
